    Questions

    Frequently asked

    What is Lily's Secret?

    We're a UAE maker of bespoke, hand-poured candles. Each one hides a secret personal message at the bottom of the glass, revealed only as the flame burns down.

    How does the secret message work?

    You choose a fragrance and write your message. We hand-set it at the bottom of the glass before the final pour, so it stays hidden in the wax and is revealed as the candle burns down.

    How do I place an order?

    Use the 'Create Your Candle' builder, then tap 'Send my order' or 'Order on WhatsApp'. No payment is taken online — we confirm every bespoke order with you personally.

    Can I return or change a candle?

    As each candle is personalised, it can't be returned once production begins. If anything arrives damaged or incorrect we'll put it right — see our Returns page.

    Where are the candles made, and do you deliver?

    Every candle is handmade to order in the UAE. We deliver across the UAE.
